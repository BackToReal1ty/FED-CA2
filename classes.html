Classes in Applied AI and Analytics

Learn how to code and manage applications with proficiency and accuracy. Whether you are a Computing student, an ad-hoc learner, or even an average joe, we have classes to cater to your needs.

Level 1
No Experience Required

This course will teach you the basics of programming, where you will learn basic languages and logic.

Level 2
No Experience Required

Here, we will focus on Analysis of data and the different analysis techniques.

Level 3
Some Experience Required

Have some experience in AI and Analytics? Here we will be learning how to gather data for analysis.

Level 4
Some Experience Required

At this level, you will learn how to use analysed data in real-world applications, and even develop a simple program!

Level 5
Moderate Experience Required

Here you will learn to create advanced applications and solve problems that may come about.

Level 6
Moderate Experience Required

At this stage, you should be relatively familiar with the subject. This course will teach you how to spot hidden errors and mistakes.
